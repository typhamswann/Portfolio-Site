Background and Motivation

A large body of research has consistently shown that the most disadvantaged Americans often face the worst environmental conditions, substantially reducing both health and lifespan. Recently, new datasets have become available, thanks to Biden and Harris administration and researchers Susanne Amelie Benz and Jennifer Anne Burney. Taking inspiration from Opportunity Insight’s “Opportunity Atlas,” I created The Environmental Equity Atlas to clearly show environmental inequality across the U.S. by allowing users to see various environmental health and socioeconomic conditions in their neighborhood.
